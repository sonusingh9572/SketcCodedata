Upload & Publisher Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This policy explains how users can upload projects and the rules they must follow on SketchCode. By contributing to our platform, you help build a better development ecosystem.

person_outline

1. Eligibility for Upload

  • Only registered users with a valid account may upload projects to SketchCode.
  • The uploader must be the original creator of the project or possess the legal authorization and rights required for public sharing.
task_alt

2. Upload Guidelines

When uploading an app, project, or source code, you must ensure the following:

  • Completeness: All shared files must be complete, functional, and ready for use.
  • Accuracy: The project title, description, and screenshots must accurately reflect the actual content of the files.
  • No Deception: Fake previews or misleading information intended to gain attention or downloads is strictly prohibited.
  • Testing: All projects must be thoroughly tested for errors and bugs before submission.
  • Formats: Only supported formats such as APK, ZIP, SWB, and other standard source files are permitted.
block

3. Prohibited Uploads

The following content types are strictly forbidden and will result in immediate removal:

  • Broken, incomplete, or corrupted files that cannot be executed.
  • Malware, spyware, viruses, or any scripts designed for harmful activities.
  • Fake projects, cloned content without modification, or low-effort "spam" uploads.
  • Copyrighted, stolen, or pirated material shared without permission.
  • Illegal, adult, or offensive content.

public Public Access & Modification Rights

By uploading to SketchCode, you acknowledge that your source code becomes publicly accessible. Users are permitted to download, edit, and modify your code for educational and development purposes.

Liability Disclaimer: The uploader is not responsible for any consequences, modifications, or misuse of the code by other users once it is public. All projects are shared for educational purposes only.

warning_amber

4. Incorrect or Misleading Uploads

  • Content that violates accuracy standards may be removed without prior notice.
  • In minor cases, the user may receive a formal warning via the platform.
  • Repeated violations of data accuracy may lead to temporary or permanent account suspension.
report

5. Report Handling & Abuse

  • Community members may report projects that are broken, fake, stolen, or unsafe.
  • Projects receiving multiple valid reports will undergo a manual investigation by the moderation team.
  • Accounts found abusing the report system or frequently uploading violating content may be permanently banned.
gavel

6. Moderation & Review

SketchCode reserves the final and discretionary right to approve, reject, edit metadata, or remove any content at any time to protect the platform's integrity.

security

7. Policy Violations

Any attempt to bypass moderation, manipulate report systems, or upload harmful content will result in immediate suspension and a permanent ban from the SketchCode Store.

update

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated at any time to reflect platform changes. Continued use of the platform and its upload features constitutes acceptance of the latest rules.
